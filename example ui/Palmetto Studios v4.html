If this is your first time here, welcome. You've landed on the home of Palmetto Studios, a creative studio that uses fashion as its medium.

What you'll find on the site: the current catalog of essentials, a lookbook, and the studio's ongoing work. New collections drop episodically rather than seasonally, each one a new world rather than a new wardrobe, so the site changes shape over time. What's here today won't be what's here next time.

If you want the deeper context, the About page covers what the studio is and how it works. Otherwise, take your time, and feel free to write to us if anything catches your eye.

About Palmetto Studiosعن ستوديوهات بالميتو _▢✕

Palmetto Studios is a creative studio that uses fashion as its primary medium. Its core practice is world-building through art direction and design, each collection is an episode, a self-contained world with its own visual language, references, and lore.

The studio operates from Saudi Arabia and is built on four ideas that show up in everything it makes: nostalgia, Arabic identity, satire, and playfulness. Drops are thematic rather than seasonal, a new collection is a new world, not a new wardrobe.

Apparel is where the studio enters, not where it ends. Palmetto is built to expand into objects, collaborations, spaces, and cultural projects.

Most fashion brands sell product. Palmetto sells worlds. The product is how you enter them.

ستوديوهات بالميتو استوديو إبداعي يتّخذ الأزياء وسيطَه الأساسي. ممارسته الجوهرية هي بناء العوالم عبر الإخراج الفني والتصميم, كل مجموعة حلقة قائمة بذاتها، عالم مستقل بلغته البصرية ومرجعياته وحكاياته.

يعمل الاستوديو من المملكة العربية السعودية، وقائم على أربع أفكار تتجلّى في كل ما يصنعه: الحنين، والهوية العربية، والسخرية، والمرح. الإصدارات موضوعاتية لا موسمية, كل مجموعة جديدة عالمٌ جديد، لا خزانة ملابس جديدة.

الأزياء هي مدخل الاستوديو، لا منتهاه. بالميتو مبنيّ ليتوسّع في الأشياء والتعاونات والفضاءات والمشاريع الثقافية.

معظم دور الأزياء تبيع منتجات. بالميتو يبيع عوالم, والمنتج هو بابك إليها.

Meaning Behind: Palmettoالمعنى وراء بالميتو _▢✕

Meaning Behind:
Palmetto

The Palmetto Tree is usually a low-growing, fan-leaved palm. The reason for choosing this as a brand name is because it represents the little kid inside of us designing, creating, and innovating through this lens. I aim to introduce playful elements, nostalgia, and a bit of satire in my design ethos, striving to create products that spark a reaction and a sense of belonging.

المعنى وراء:
بالميتو

شجرة البالميتو عادةً نخلةٌ قصيرة بأوراق مروحيّة الشكل. سبب اختيارها اسماً للعلامة هو أنّها تمثّل الطفل الصغير الذي يسكن داخلنا, يصمّم ويبتكر ويُبدع عبر هذه العدسة. أسعى إلى إدخال عناصر مرحة، والحنين، وقدر من السخرية في أسلوبي التصميمي، ساعياً لصنع منتجات تُثير ردّ فعل وإحساساً بالانتماء.

Wheel of Fortune: Symbol of Wealth in Persian Carpetry
عجلة الحظّ, رمز الوفرة في فنّ السجّاد الفارسي

The new logo is inspired by the ornaments of a Persian rug. The spiral shape in these ornaments often symbolizes the blessing of wealth and abundance. Hopefully, my brand will give you that feeling of blessing and importance.

Returnsالاسترجاع _▢✕

Returns are available for orders shipped within Saudi Arabia.

You have 30 days from the date your order arrives to return it. Items must be unworn, unwashed, with original tags attached. Return shipping is on us, write to us and we'll arrange the pickup. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days of the item arriving back at the studio.

International orders are final sale. If something arrives damaged or incorrect, write to us and we'll make it right.
